# Auto Pilot TikTok - Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026.

Auto Pilot TikTok, the app, helps the account owner schedule and publish their own short videos to their connected TikTok account.

## Information we access

With your permission via TikTok Login, the app accesses your basic profile information such as open ID, display name, and avatar, plus an access token that allows the app to publish videos to your account.

## How we use it

The information is used only to upload and publish the videos you provide to your own TikTok account at the times you schedule. The app does not post content from any other person.

## Sharing

We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

## Storage and control

Access tokens are stored securely and are removed when you disconnect. You can revoke access for the app at any time in your TikTok account settings.
